# Data Science Assignment – Web3 Trading Team

## Objective
Analyze how trader behavior aligns or diverges from overall Bitcoin market sentiment
(Fear vs Greed) using historical trader data and the Fear & Greed Index.

The analysis focuses on understanding changes in:
- Profitability
- Risk exposure and leverage
- Position sizing
- Trading volume
- Directional bias (Long vs Short)

---

## Dataset Used
1. **Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index**
   - Daily sentiment indicator representing overall market psychology

2. **Hyperliquid Historical Trader Data**
   - Trade-level data including execution price, position size, closed PnL,
     direction, leverage proxy, and timestamps
